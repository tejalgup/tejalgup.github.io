Throughout my undergraduate career, I was extremely lucky with the chance to perform undergraduate research. The econmics department was looking for somebody with highly technical skills to help on a project. I got the chance to talk with [Dr. Allison Shertzer](https://www.allisonshertzer.com/), the professor looking for assistants, after and luckily for me she was impressed with my background. I started doing trainings and learning about maps and editing using ArcGIS officially joining the project in February 2022 (on Valentine's day hehe.)

Throughout my time with her, I worked on old land-use maps and edited them according to land zones. After a full year of working for her, I ended up leaving when I took a gap semester for school to join P&G in January 2023.
