Do you ever wonder who you'll end up with? Constantly it runs through my mind. But I'm at the point where I want to wait and really take my time choosing somebody to get close to my heart. Somebody I can trust and who I know is right for me. I'm used to sticking with people I don't see a long-term future with because I was lonely, but now I want to learn what it's like to be single. To not look for anything in any capacity. No more apps, no more daydreaming about people on the street, I just want to live my life on my own for once. But that doesn't mean I'll ever stop dreaming....
This is a letter for the future, for my hope

--------------------------------------------------------------------

Dear Future Husband,

That’s a crazy line to say- very Megan Trainor. I don’t know why I’m writing this. Maybe it’ll be something cute to look back on. It’ll give you a chance to know who I really am before I meet you. But to be completely honest right now, I’m trying to work on myself so I can figure out who I am. And when I meet you, I want to stay true to that. 
Who I am? I’m sure you already know that if you’re reading this. But who I am right now? I’m 21 years old, and I’m a month away from graduation still trying to figure out where I’m going to end up. I recently got out of a relationship and am on my own for the first time. It was really scary at first, but now I really like it. I want to focus on what my life is like without constantly searching for people to have connections with short or long term and forcing wrong things to be right. I see all my friends being happily single, not on the apps or anything, and I wondered a lot how they didn’t get lonely. Somehow those friends are the people who are best suited for relationships in my opinion. Happiness doesn’t come from relationships; it comes from oneself. I’ve never been alone, but now is the time when I can finally watch the movies I want, live on my schedule, focus on my friends and career and learn about what I want for the future.

Some things about me: I love relaxing in bed and watching TV and movies. I ALWAYS wear pajamas- it’s the first thing I do when I get home! I love eating little yummy treats: candy, ice cream, boba and donuts are some of my favorite little snicc snaccs. I love listening and playing the piano. I also like knitting and crocheting little yarn thingies! And maybe the most important things about me- I’m a hopeless romantic. I love playing dating simulators, watching soap operas, and reading cheesy romance novels. I love love. That’s why, when I find you, I want to be sure and true to my heart when I pick you. 

Now who are you? I’m not exactly sure. I’m a pretty flexible person when it comes to  who I’ll date. I think I’ve figured out that I am indeed straight, so I’m assuming you’re a man lol. Probably near my age since I find that to be important. But beyond that? I’m not sure. I hope you’ll be patient and look for the best in any situation . And kind to not just me, but everybody around you. I know you’ll be sure that I’m the person for you and that you are the person for me. I also know you’ll be a genuine joy in my life when I eventually find you. I don’t know when that’ll be- maybe a year or three from now. But when I find you, we’ll know it’s meant to be us.

I wonder what we’ll be like. I think you’ll be the type of boy to buy me flowers and take me on cute dates while I crochet you cute silly things. We can go to the park and hammock together. I’ll read my silly romance novels and you’ll read…. I have no clue! We’ll go out to parties and have fun movie nights in. I think you’ll be the type of person who gets along with my friends and me with yours like we’re meant to be part of each other’s lives. We’ll have fun gaming nights together! I think you’re going to be the person who will want to hold me when I’m sad no matter what and put my feelings above your own. You’ll understand the things I’ve been through and assure me that I’m strong enough to get through it. I’ll be somebody who you can be yourself to and tell me anything, and I’ll tell you everything in my heart. You will want me and I’ll want you. And we’ll be sweet and sappy together.
Well, that all sounds like a really good deal for me but what about you? I’ll always fight for you and our love. I am a person who doesn’t give up and is insanely loyal. And when it comes to somebody I care about, I will not compromise. I’ll be there when you need somebody to cry to and listen to whatever you have to say. You’ll be the person I always think about running through my mind. I’ll come up with cute plans to show how much I’m thinking about you. I’ll give you all of me.

This is the only general letter I’ll write to you. After this I’ll make them niche topics about my life!

Whoever you are, I’ll love you before you know it,
Your future wife
